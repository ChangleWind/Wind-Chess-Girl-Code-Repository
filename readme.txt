作者的信

亲爱的开发者同好们：
我是常乐风，这个Galgame游戏框架的作者。提笔之际，正值新春，先祝各位春节快乐——虽然这祝福可能比代码里的bug还虚（笑）。
这个框架是我一时兴起的产物，最初设想的功能挺多，但实际实现后，bug也如影随形。坦率说，我已经懒得修复了，毕竟热情来得快，去得也快。不过，为了方便后来者，我在代码中尽量采用了清晰的注释和变量名，希望能为你们的二次开发省点力气。
框架里内置了一种我自创的棋类游戏“风棋”，算是个人恶趣味的小实验。剧本方面，则受了山本崇一郎《即便如此依旧步步紧逼》的启发——那股青涩又执拗的氛围，我很想用游戏再现几分。
如果你对这个框架感兴趣，欢迎关注我的B站账户 @蝉鸣社-常乐风，虽然更新随缘，但或许会有意外收获。对了，不必谢我（毕竟连bug都没修），权当是开源社区的随手分享吧。
如果能把这些bug修一修，再补上音乐和美术资源，它或许真能成为一个在Steam上卖6块钱的小游戏。就像我常说的：这玩意儿只为博君一笑，不必照单全收。未尽事宜，请参阅框架附带的声明文件。
驻春节快乐，bug没有（当然，这个“功能”我显然没实现）。
常乐风
中华人民共和国北京
丙午年正月初一（农历新年）
附注：如果你真的基于这个框架做出了什么，记得告诉我——我会是你第一个玩家。

English Translation:
Author's Letter
Dear Fellow Developers,
I am Chang Lefeng, the creator of this Galgame framework. As I write this, it's the Spring Festival, so first, happy Lunar New Year—though this wish might be emptier than the bugs in the code (laughs).
This framework was born out of a sudden impulse. I initially envisioned many features, but after implementation, bugs followed closely behind. Honestly, I can't be bothered to fix them anymore—enthusiasm comes and goes quickly. However, for the convenience of future developers, I've tried to use clear comments and variable names in the code, hoping to save you some effort during secondary development.
The framework includes a self-created board game called "Wind Chess," a small experiment born from my personal quirks. As for the script, it was inspired by Yamamoto Souichirou's Even So, I Press Forward—I wanted to recreate a bit of that awkward yet persistent atmosphere in the game.
If you're interested in this framework, feel free to follow my Bilibili account @蝉鸣社-常乐风 (Cicada Studio - Chang Lefeng). Updates are sporadic, but you might find some unexpected content there. Oh, and no need to thank me (after all, I haven't even fixed the bugs). Consider it a casual contribution to the open-source community.
If you manage to fix these bugs and add music and art assets, it might even become a small game worth ¥6 on Steam. As I often say: this thing is just for fun, so don't take it too seriously. For anything not covered here, please refer to the statement document included with the framework.
Wishing you a Spring Festival free of bugs (though, admittedly, this "feature" is one I clearly didn't implement).
Chang Lefeng
Beijing, People's Republic of China
Lunar New Year's Day, Year of the Fire Horse (February 17, 2026)
P.S. If you actually create something based on this framework, be sure to let me know—I'll be your first player.
